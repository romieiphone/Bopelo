About Us

Our Journey

Lindiwe Tylampi, the Founder of Bopelo was born and breed in a small mining village in the North West. She was raised by her Father who was a miner and Mother, a housewife and has a younger brother. Irrespective of their background her father is her inspiration who grew them up strong and independent.
Lindiwe came to Johannesburg to pursue her dreams and for higher studies. To meet the ends she worked at every possible place and every possible work like in Restaurants, doing housekeeping and about any job she could find. Regardless of my situation I managed to help people who were in need more than her while she thanked the almighty Giver to life and Hope, to instil faith and unconditional love towards everyone and to every challenge she went through for she knew there was no day that went pass without learning and growing from that situation, whether it be good or bad. These lessons of life broadened her vision and perspective to life and hence Bopelo came to life. The essence of knowing that nothing is impossible in life when you have Hope, and we at Bopelo try to teach this on a daily basis. 

Mission Statement

“The birds of hope are everywhere, listen to them sing.” -Terri Guillemets
The mission of Bopelo is to provide a continuum of hope related services to the people of South Africa, thereby restoring hope with the people to come out of the darkness of despair. 

Vision Statement

To become South Africa’s catalyst for change by pushing the boundaries to realize transformation in the best possible way.
